Bloody duck is one of the most traditional local dishes in Hunan's Xiangxi. It is made from duck blood and glutinous rice. Its tastes spicy and delicious.

Miao sour fish soup has been summed up by Hmong people in the long life of a delicacy. Its production method is simple, a delicious food after the appetizer.

Ginger sugar is a popular snack in Fenghuang. It is a special sugar made from sugar and ginger, allowing you to taste both sweetness from the sugar and the spiciness of the ginger.
